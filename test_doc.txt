Employee Onboarding Checklist

Step 1: Pre-Arrival Preparation
- Confirm start date with candidate
- Arrange office space and equipment
- Create email account and systems access
- Prepare welcome kit with company information

Step 2: First Day Orientation
- Meet with HR for paperwork completion
- Complete I-9 verification
- Review company policies and code of conduct
- Discuss benefits enrollment options
- Take security and compliance training

Step 3: Department Onboarding
- Meet with direct manager and team
- Review role responsibilities and expectations
- Set up systems access (VPN, repositories, applications)
- Assign initial projects and learning materials
- Schedule regular check-in meetings

Step 4: First Week Activities
- Complete mandatory compliance training
- Attend company-wide orientation session
- Participate in team building activities
- Begin knowledge transfer sessions
- Establish communication preferences

Step 5: First Month Goals
- Complete all initial training modules
- Deliver first meaningful contribution to project
- Establish relationships with key team members
- Provide feedback on onboarding experience
- Schedule 30-day review meeting
